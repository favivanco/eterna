Cellular Reprogramming Shows Promise for Age Reversal

Scientists reversed aging markers in human cells using careful reprogramming.

Why this matters: may restore youthful function without raising cancer risk.

Mediterranean Diet Linked to 20% Lower Mortality

Long-term study highlights olive oil, nuts, and fish as helpful staples.

Why this matters: clear dietary guidance for long healthspan.

NAD+ Precursor Shows Cognitive Benefits

Why this matters: first human evidence for brain health effect.

About Eterna

Eterna is a learning and self-reflection project focused on human longevity. Our mission is to make the science of longer, healthier lives easy to understand and emotionally meaningful. We build simple tools that: (1) teach core ideas from credible research, (2) help people notice how everyday choices affect their healthspan, and (3) encourage thoughtful conversations about fairness, ethics, access, and purpose. Eterna does not provide medical advice.
